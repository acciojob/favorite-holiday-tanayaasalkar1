Maldives


The Maldives is one of the world's most beautiful holiday destinations, known for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, white sandy beaches, and luxurious overwater villas. Located in the Indian Ocean, it consists of more than 1,000 coral islands grouped into 26 atolls. Visitors can enjoy activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, swimming, and relaxing by the beach while exploring vibrant marine life. With its peaceful atmosphere, breathtaking sunsets, and warm hospitality, the Maldives is a perfect destination for couples, families, and anyone seeking a relaxing tropical vacation.

  1. Male – The vibrant capital, known for its local markets, mosques, and cultural attractions.
  2. Banana Reef – A world-famous scuba diving and snorkeling spot with colorful coral reefs and marine life.
  3. Vaadhoo Island – Famous for the "Sea of Stars," where the beach glows at night due to bioluminescent plankton.
  4. Maafushi – A popular local island offering affordable resorts, water sports, and beautiful beaches.
  5. Hulhumale – Known for its clean beaches, parks, cafés, and water activities.
  • Mas Huni – A traditional breakfast made with shredded tuna, coconut, onion, and chili, served with flatbread.
  • Garudhiya – A simple and flavorful fish soup served with rice, lime, onion, and chili.
  • Fihunu Mas – Fresh fish marinated with local spices and grilled to perfection.
  • Bis Keemiya – A crispy pastry filled with tuna, cabbage, eggs, and onions.
  • Saagu Bondibai – A sweet sago pudding cooked with coconut milk, sugar, and cardamom.
