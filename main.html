Maldives


The Maldives is one of the world's most beautiful holiday destinations, known for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, white sandy beaches, and luxurious overwater villas.

  1. Male - Capital city
  2. Banana Reef - Famous diving spot
  3. Vaadhoo Island - Sea of Stars
  4. Maafushi - Beautiful beaches
  5. Hulhumale - Artificial island
  • Mas Huni - Traditional breakfast
  • Garudhiya - Fish soup
  • Fihunu Mas - Grilled fish
  • Bis Keemiya - Stuffed pastry
  • Saagu Bondibai - Sweet dessert
